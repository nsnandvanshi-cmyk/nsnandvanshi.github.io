Velvet Scars follows Elara Vance, a talented yet broken artist, and Damien Kade, a ruthless billionaire with a dark past. 
Their intense and dangerous attraction pulls them into a world of power, betrayal, and obsession, where every decision could lead to destruction. 
As their hidden pasts collide, they are forced to confront the scars they've both tried to bury, unraveling secrets that threaten to tear them apart. 
In a high-stakes game of love and lies, they must decide whether they can heal or if the darkness will consume them both.
